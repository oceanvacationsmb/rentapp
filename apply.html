Important Credit Report Notice

To help speed up the application process and avoid additional screening fees, applicants are required to upload a recent credit report PDF or clear screenshots from one of the following:

  • Credit Karma
  • Experian
  • Equifax
  • TransUnion
  • AnnualCreditReport.com

The report must belong to the applicant, show the applicant’s name, and be dated within the last 30 days.

If a recent credit report is not provided, applicants may be required to complete a separate credit/background screening through a third-party provider at an additional cost of approximately $50.
